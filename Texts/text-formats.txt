DrScheme uses platform specific text formats. On the PC this means that
texts with special language specific characters, such as norwegian
ae-ligature, o-slash and a-ring, must be in UTF-8 format, whereas text
with standard english characters only, may be in ANSI format.


The following 9 texts are in ANSI format

trette-netter
little-red-house
aftenpostenkronikker
being-earnest
through-looking-glass
aladdin-und-lampe
cardello
La-Nina-de-Luzmela
bellum-gallicum
